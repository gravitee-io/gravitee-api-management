# Meetings Directory

This directory stores meeting notes for Epic `APIM-10345 Member Mapping to Application`.

Use it for working sessions where the team wants to:
- prepare a meeting agenda around story scope, requirements, or implementation direction
- capture open questions to resolve with Product, Design, or Engineering
- record the decisions that were made during the meeting
- keep a lightweight trace of why scope or wording changed over time

Meeting notes in this directory should use filenames with a date prefix and a short slug:
- `YYYY-MM-DD-<short-slug>.md`
- Example: `2026-03-23-story-clarification-session.md`
- Example: `2026-03-24-invitation-status-review.md`

Each meeting note should usually include:
- the meeting date
- the goal of the meeting
- the agenda or topics to review
- the decisions made
- open questions, if any remain
- concrete follow-up actions or owners when needed

Meeting notes do not replace project decision records under `../decisions/`.
If a meeting produces a durable Epic-level decision, also capture the final outcome as a decision record in `../decisions/`.
